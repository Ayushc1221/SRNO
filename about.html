Understanding Our Organization and Mission

Our Vision

Overview

SRNO INDIA LLP is a limited liability partnership business entity registered under the LLP Act, 2008 (formerly known as Samajik Shodh Network Sansthan). Headquartered at Lucknow (India), SRNO INDIA LLP is a leading professional organization involved in rendering professional services to international agencies and government departments across various development sectors.

SRNO has successfully executed many assignments including large-scale surveys, monitoring and evaluation studies, capacity building and training programs. The organization provides implementation support to various Government and Non-Government Organizations and has also developed core competencies in the areas of research and consultancy.

We pride ourselves on being one of the country's leading firms specializing in survey, research, analysis, and capacity building. With a strong track record of delivering high-quality results, our organization is dedicated to driving meaningful insights and impactful solutions across India.
