Our Team

The People Behind Our Success

Promoted by professionals having vast experience in the social development sector, SRNO possesses a unique capability and resources to plan and execute projects in varied fields. The team of the organization comprises of highly skilled and dedicated individuals.

Founder

Brijendra Singh
Founder
Mr. Singh has rich professional experience in planning & moduling of various socio-economic research projects and training & capacity building programs, across a wide range of social sectors. Young, energetic and a good team leader, Mr. Singh has been able to implement large scale surveys in a time bound and most satisfactory manner. Possessing professional experience of more than 15 years, he has been instrumental in completing the entire field work for GATS-9, Annual Health Survey, DLHS-3, DLHS-4 and NFHS-4. In 2016, Mr. Singh established SRNO India to provide knowledge-based services to various State & Central Government organizations.
